# MCEM Stage Accountability Matrix

## Overview

This document details the Microsoft Customer Engagement Methodology (MCEM) stage accountability, including lead roles, supporting roles, and specific activities for each role across all five stages.

---

## Stage Summary

| Stage | Lead Role | Supporting Roles |
|-------|-----------|------------------|
| Listen & Consult | AE/ATS | SSP, CSAM, SAE, Partner |
| Inspire & Design | SSP/SE | AE, ATS, SAE, PSS/PDM |
| Empower & Achieve | SSP/SE | AE, ATS, CE, SAE, PSS/PDM |
| Realize Value | CSAM/CSA | SAE, Services, Partner, AE, ATS |
| Manage & Optimize | CSAM/CSA | SAE, Services, Partner, AE, ATS |

---

## Detailed Role Activities by Stage

### 1. AE (Account Executive)

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | Develop account plan, meet customer & qualify, engages partner |
| **Inspire & Design** | Orchestrate pursuit team |
| **Empower & Achieve** | Facilitate negotiation and deal closure |
| **Realize Value** | — |
| **Manage & Optimize** | — |

---

### 2. ATS (Account Technology Strategist)

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | Develop Customer Secure AI Assessment, CxO TDM relationship, MACC Consumption Plan |
| **Inspire & Design** | Drive tech efforts, ensure solution fits customer tech landscape and Customer Secure AI Assessment |
| **Empower & Achieve** | Secure tech agreements, ensures consumption plan covers at least 1st year of MACC value |
| **Realize Value** | — |
| **Manage & Optimize** | — |

**Additional (IA - Industry Advisor):**
- Listen & Consult: Create and qualify industry use case pipeline in partnership with AE for CSA and SDC/ISV

---

### 3. SSP (Solution Sales Professional)

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | Support qualification, identify Solution Play |
| **Inspire & Design** | Execute Solution Play, engage Partner and/or Services |
| **Empower & Achieve** | Secure business decision |
| **Realize Value** | — |
| **Manage & Optimize** | — |

---

### 4. SE (Solution Engineer) / CSA (Cloud Solution Architect)

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | — |
| **Inspire & Design** | Technical assessment, initial architecture, and proof |
| **Empower & Achieve** | Secure technical decision |
| **Realize Value** | Drive delivery and consumption/usage for committed milestones |
| **Manage & Optimize** | Drive solution health, identify expansion opportunities |

**Hub SE Activities:**
- Inspire & Design: Engagement journey mapped to Secure AI Plan
- Empower & Achieve: Secure technical decision

---

### 5. CSAM (Customer Success Account Manager)

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | — |
| **Inspire & Design** | Create Customer Success Plan (CSP) |
| **Empower & Achieve** | Prepare for milestone transition to delivery, validate and confirm CSP |
| **Realize Value** | Coordinate CSP delivery, lead committed milestones to completion (Unified). Engages CSA as needed |
| **Manage & Optimize** | Achieve customer health outcomes, accelerate customer value and identify expansion opportunities |

---

### 6. CE (Commercial Executive)

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | — |
| **Inspire & Design** | — |
| **Empower & Achieve** | Drive deal strategy, negotiation and deal execution |
| **Realize Value** | — |
| **Manage & Optimize** | — |

---

### 7. SAE (Services Account Executive)

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | Meet customer and qualify services opportunity (Consulting + Unified), contributes to AP |
| **Inspire & Design** | Orchestrate Services teams with support from Custom Consulting Sales teams or Unified GBB |
| **Empower & Achieve** | Drives negotiation and services deal closure |
| **Realize Value** | Stays engaged with Consulting CPM or Unified CSDR activities during project delivery |
| **Manage & Optimize** | Identifies services expansion opportunities |

---

### 8. Partner / PSS / PDM

| Stage | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | Partner: Discover Co-sell opportunities, submit inbound referral |
| **Inspire & Design** | PSS/PDM: Accept outbound opportunities, collaborate on planning, leverage incentives |
| **Empower & Achieve** | PSS/PDM: Deliver PoC, business value, accelerate & secure agreement |
| **Realize Value** | Partner: Drive delivery of partner delivered milestones |
| **Manage & Optimize** | Partner: Optimize solution architecture, identify expansion opportunities |

> **Note:** When PSS is not in place, PDM is accountable.

---

## Exit Criteria by Stage

| Stage | Exit Criteria |
|-------|---------------|
| **Listen & Consult** | Qualified pipeline |
| **Inspire & Design** | Customer aligned to solution and business case |
| **Empower & Achieve** | Committed pipeline and customer agreement |
| **Realize Value** | Outcomes met and baseline metrics in place |
| **Manage & Optimize** | Completed opportunity, next steps identified |

| **STU** | Solution Team Unit | SSP, SE | Inspire & Design, Empower & Achieve |
| **CSU** | Customer Success Unit | CSAM, CSA | Realize Value, Manage & Optimize |
