# MCEM Sales Roles Guide

## Overview

**MCEM (Microsoft Customer Engagement Methodology)** is Microsoft's unified framework for engaging customers across the entire lifecycle. It connects all customer-facing roles (sales, customer success, industry solutions, and partners) into one team to deliver consistent, customer-centric engagements that drive business outcomes.

MCEM is central to **MCAPS (Microsoft Customer and Partner Solutions)** motions and comprises five stages: **Listen & Consult, Inspire & Design, Empower & Achieve, Realize Value, and Manage & Optimize**.

### Commercial Executives (CE)
Drive deal strategy, negotiations, and deal execution.

### Solution Engineers (SE)
Drive the technical win to accelerate customer commitment.

---

## Accountable Units

The MCEM journey stages are owned by three accountable units:

### ATU (Account Team Unit)
**Roles:** AE (Account Executive), ATS (Account Technology Strategist)

**Accountable for:**
- **Listen & Consult** stage
- Strategic planning & execution for accounts

### STU (Solution Team Unit)
**Roles:** SSP (Solution Sales Professional), SE (Solution Engineer)

**Accountable for:**
- **Inspire & Design** stage
- **Empower & Achieve** stage

### CSU (Customer Success Unit)
**Roles:** CSAM (Customer Success Account Manager), CSA (Cloud Solution Architect)

**Accountable for:**
- **Realize Value** stage
- **Manage & Optimize** stage

---

## MCEM Journey Stages & Exit Criteria

| Stage | Exit Criteria | Accountable Unit |
|-------|---------------|------------------|
| Listen & Consult | Qualified pipeline | ATU |
| Inspire & Design | Customer aligned to solution and business case | STU |
| Empower & Achieve | Committed pipeline and customer agreement | STU |
| Realize Value | Outcomes met and baseline metrics in place | CSU |
| Manage & Optimize | Completed opportunity, next steps identified | CSU |

## Role Definitions

### 1. SSP (Solution Sales Professional)

**Focus:** Drives solution-specific sales motions.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Qualify opportunities and execute Solution Plays
- Engage partners and/or services for technical validation
- Secure the business decision for the solution area
- Collaborate with Account Executive and technical specialists during early stages of the deal

**MCEM Focus:** Compete, Expand (solution-specific opportunities)

---

### 2. AE (Account Executive)

**Focus:** Owns the overall account strategy and executive relationships.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Lead account planning and orchestration across the v-team
- Understand customer business challenges and define strategy
- Drive negotiation and deal closure
- Act as the "quarterback" for the account, ensuring alignment between business outcomes and Microsoft solutions

**MCEM Focus:** All stages - primary orchestrator of the MCEM engagement

---

### 3. ATS (Account Technology Strategist)

**Focus:** Owns the technology and innovation strategy for the account.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Map business value to technology capabilities
- Develop technical roadmaps and secure technical agreements
- Ensure solutions fit the customer's tech landscape and consumption plan
- Partner with AE to align technology strategy with business priorities

**MCEM Focus:** Map (technical discovery), Compete (technical differentiation), Modernize (architecture)

---

### 4. CSA (Cloud Solution Architect)

**Focus:** Technical delivery and consumption.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Drive implementation and usage for committed milestones
- Ensure solution health and identify expansion opportunities
- Maintain Customer Success Plan (CSP) status and RAID logs
- Validate outcomes and recommend next steps post-delivery

**MCEM Focus:** Modernize (implementation), Expand (consumption-based growth)

---

### 5. CSAM (Customer Success Account Manager)

**Focus:** Orchestrates customer success and value realization.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Create and manage the Customer Success Plan
- Coordinate delivery of milestones and ensure operational health
- Accelerate customer value and identify expansion opportunities
- Engage executive sponsors and manage strategic account success rhythms

**MCEM Focus:** All stages post-sale - ensures long-term value realization

---

## How They Work Together

| Collaboration | Purpose |
|---------------|---------|
| **AE + ATS** | Define account and technology strategy |
| **SSP** | Executes solution-specific sales plays |
| **CSA** | Delivers technical outcomes and drives consumption |
| **CSAM** | Ensures long-term success and value realization post-sale |

## MCEM Stage Role Assignments

### MAP Stage
Primary roles involved in customer landscape discovery and strategy definition.
- **AE** - Lead relationship mapping and stakeholder identification
- **ATS** - Conduct technical discovery
- **CSAM** - Identify business outcomes and success criteria

### COMPETE Stage
Primary roles involved in competitive positioning and winning the deal.
- **SSP** - Provide workload-specific competitive intelligence
- **ATS** - Address technical differentiation
- **AE** - Develop overall win strategy

### EXPAND Stage
Primary roles involved in identifying growth and upsell opportunities.
- **AE** - Identify account expansion opportunities
- **SSP** - Discover workload-specific upsell possibilities
- **CSAM** - Identify adoption opportunities and additional use cases
- **CSA** - Identify consumption-based expansion

### MODERNIZE Stage
Primary roles involved in cloud migration and digital transformation.
- **ATS** - Design cloud architecture and migration strategy
- **SSP** - Position modern workload capabilities
- **CSA** - Drive technical implementation and consumption
- **CSAM** - Create transformation vision and change management plan
